NOVA MARKET ULTRA — READ ME FIRST

What this template is:
Nova Market Ultra is a polished multi-page storefront with a built-in seller dashboard, order view, payout snapshot, inventory alerts, and inbox preview, customer account area, account sign-in included starter roles, theme settings page, and easy local owner controls for the store brand.

Best setup order:
1. Open account.html and sign in with a included owner or seller account.
2. Open site-settings.html and set the store name, logo, announcement bar, utility note, social links, and theme.
3. Open seller-dashboard.html and add or edit products.
4. Replace the included product and category images inside the images folder when needed.
5. Test cart, wishlist, checkout, account, tracking, and contact.
6. Publish only after the contact form path and store policies are updated for the real business.

Built-in starter accounts:
Owner: owner@novamarket.example / owner123
Seller: seller@novamarket.example / seller123
Shopper: shopper@novamarket.example / shopper123

Most important pages:
index.html — home page
shop.html — product catalog
collections.html — category browsing
deals.html — sale page
seller-dashboard.html — add, edit, delete products
account.html — sign in starter accounts and manage shopper info
site-settings.html — store brand, logo, socials, and theme controls
start-here.html — setup order in plain language

What saves where:
This version saves products, cart data, account data, brand settings, and theme settings in the browser on the device being used. It is a front-end product, not a live cloud backend.

If you need a future backend:
This project can be upgraded later to Supabase for real auth, storage, and store-wide saved settings.
