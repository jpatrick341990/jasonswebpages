FORM SETUP GUIDE

The contact form is in demo mode until you connect a real form service.

---------------------------------
1. FILE TO EDIT
---------------------------------
contact.html

---------------------------------
2. WHAT TO FIND
---------------------------------
Find the form tag.
It currently looks like this:
<form class="styled-form" action="#" method="post">

---------------------------------
3. WHAT TO CHANGE
---------------------------------
Replace the # with your real form endpoint.

Example with Formspree:
<form class="styled-form" action="https://formspree.io/f/yourformid" method="post">

---------------------------------
4. WHY THIS MATTERS
---------------------------------
If the action is # or blank, the template stays in demo mode.
The form will show a demo message instead of sending anything.

---------------------------------
5. TEST BEFORE PUBLISHING
---------------------------------
After adding your real form action:
- save contact.html
- reload the page
- send a test inquiry
- make sure it reaches your email or form inbox

---------------------------------
6. SIMPLE FORM SERVICES PEOPLE OFTEN USE
---------------------------------
- Formspree
- Basin
- Getform
- Netlify Forms (if hosting supports it)
