FORM SETUP GUIDE

This template ships with the contact form in demo mode.
That means it shows a message instead of sending real email.

To make it live:
1. Open index.html and contact.html.
2. Find the main form with id="contact-form".
3. Replace the demo form setup with your preferred service such as Formspree or Basin.
4. Remove data-demo-form="true" after you connect the real form endpoint.
5. Test the form before publishing.
