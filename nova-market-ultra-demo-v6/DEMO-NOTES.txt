NOVA MARKET ULTRA — DEMO PREVIEW NOTES

This preview build is for jasonswebpages.com.

What is different in this demo:
1. The account page keeps the access flow visible.
2. Owner, Seller, and Shopper each open with one click.
3. No real password is required in this preview build.
4. Store settings, theme changes, and seller changes save in this browser only.
5. This demo includes the Jason'sWebPages footer logo button linking back to https://jasonswebpages.com.

Best demo path:
1. Open account.html.
2. Click Owner View to explore site settings.
3. Click Seller View to explore the seller dashboard.
4. Click Shopper View to explore the account/cart/store side.
5. Use Clear preview access when you want to reset the role.
