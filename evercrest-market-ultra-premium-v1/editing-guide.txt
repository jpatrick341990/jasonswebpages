NOVA MARKET ULTRA — EDITING GUIDE

Quick edit paths:
- Brand + social links: site-settings.html
- Theme colors, fonts, spacing, and card style: site-settings.html
- Product catalog and seller listings: seller-dashboard.html
- Default starter product data: js/catalog.js
- Main styling: css/style.css and css/responsive.css
- Store scripts: js/site.js, js/theme.js, js/brand.js, js/auth.js
- Store images: images/

Missing files fixed in this upgraded build:
- js/auth.js was added

How the premium controls work:
- Site settings controls the store name, logo, announcement bar, utility bar, footer intro note, support contact info, and social links.
- Theme settings controls the look and feel of the storefront through browser-saved settings.
- Seller dashboard controls custom product listings.

Contact form note:
The contact page still works as a front-end demo by saving messages locally in this browser. For real inbox delivery, connect a form service or backend later.
