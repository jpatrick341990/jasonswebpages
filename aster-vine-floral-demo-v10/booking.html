Can this page work for workshops too?

Yes. Buyers can rename project types, swap the budget wording, and turn the page into a class booking form or group workshop inquiry.

What if the studio does not want online product ordering?

That is fine. The homepage can lean more heavily into booking, portfolio, and services while the shop page is simplified or hidden.

Why keep booking separate from contact?

Because a custom floral project usually needs a better form than a general question. Splitting them makes the business feel more organized and more established.
