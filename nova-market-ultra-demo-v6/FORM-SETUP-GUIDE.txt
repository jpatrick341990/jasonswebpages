FORM SETUP GUIDE

Current behavior:
The contact form is a browser-saved preview. It lets a buyer test the template without a backend.

For a real live site:
1. Open contact.html.
2. Replace the starter form action with your preferred form endpoint or custom backend.
3. Test one message before publishing.
4. Update the support email and phone in site-settings.html.

Simple options:
- Formspree
- Basin
- Netlify Forms
- Your own backend or serverless function

Tip:
Keep the front-end layout exactly the same. Only swap the submission target so you do not break the styling.
